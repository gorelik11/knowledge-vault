**Date:** __________ 2026

To Whom It May Concern,

On behalf of Blues Club Gdynia, I hereby confirm our interest in hosting a performance of the project "Kolot — The Voices: Home," an international music coproduction led by Warsaw-based composer Dmitry Gorelik, in partnership with Berlin-based composer and percussionist Tom Dayan, supported by the International Coproduction Fund (IKF) of the Goethe-Institut.

Blues Club Gdynia is a live music venue seating 85 guests, equipped with professional sound and lighting systems, dedicated to presenting jazz, blues, and world music.

We intend to provide the following:

- Venue for a concert performance of "Kolot — The Voices: Home"
- Technical infrastructure: sound system, lighting, and stage facilities
- Accommodation for the ensemble
- Promotional support through our channels

We look forward to presenting this project at our venue.
