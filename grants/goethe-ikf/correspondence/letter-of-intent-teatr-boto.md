**LETTER OF INTENT**

**From:** Teatr BOTO, Sopot, Poland
**Re:** "Kolot — The Voices: Home" — International Music Coproduction
**Date:** 26.03.2026

---

To Whom It May Concern,

On behalf of Teatr BOTO in Sopot, I hereby confirm our interest in hosting a performance of the project "Kolot — The Voices: Home," an international music coproduction led by Warsaw-based composer Dmitry Gorelik, in partnership with Berlin-based composer and percussionist Tom Dayan, supported by the International Coproduction Fund (IKF) of the Goethe-Institut.

Teatr BOTO is an intimate performance venue in Sopot with a 55-seat auditorium, dedicated to presenting live music, theatre, and interdisciplinary artistic events.

We intend to provide the following:

- Venue for a concert performance of "Kolot — The Voices: Home"
- Technical infrastructure: sound, lighting, and front-of-house services
- Promotional support through our channels
- Box office management

We look forward to presenting this project at our venue.

Sincerely,

Volha Kulikouskaya
Teatr BOTO
Sopot, Poland
