**LETTER OF INTENT**

**From:** Skład Butelek, Warsaw, Poland
**Re:** "Kolot — The Voices: Home" — International Music Coproduction
**Date:** 26.3 2026

---

To Whom It May Concern,

On behalf of Skład Butelek in Warsaw, I hereby confirm our interest in hosting a performance of the project "Kolot — The Voices: Home," an international music coproduction led by Warsaw-based composer Dmitry Gorelik, in partnership with Berlin-based composer and percussionist Tom Dayan, supported by the International Coproduction Fund (IKF) of the Goethe-Institut.

We intend to provide the following:

- Venue for a concert performance of "Kolot — The Voices: Home" (90-minute programme)
- Technical infrastructure: sound system, lighting, and stage facilities
- Promotion of the event through our channels
- Box office management

We look forward to presenting this project at our venue.

Sincerely,

Magdalena Wożniak
Skład Butelek
Warsaw, Poland
