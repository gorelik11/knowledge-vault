**LETTER OF INTENT**

**From:** Fundacja Integracji Kultury, Gliwice, Poland
**Re:** "Kolot — The Voices: Home" — International Music Coproduction
**Date:** __________ 2026

---

To Whom It May Concern,

I, Krzysztof Kobyliński, President of Fundacja Integracji Kultury, hereby confirm our interest in hosting the premiere performance of the project "Kolot — The Voices: Home" at Centrum Kultury Jazovia in Gliwice, Poland.

"Kolot — The Voices: Home" is an international music coproduction led by Dmitry Gorelik (Warsaw, Poland) in partnership with Berlin-based composer and percussionist Tom Dayan, supported by the International Coproduction Fund (IKF) of the Goethe-Institut.

Centrum Kultury Jazovia is a leading cultural venue in the Silesia region, dedicated to promoting jazz, world music, and intercultural artistic exchange. We have a long-standing track record of presenting international projects and collaborating with cultural institutions, including the Goethe-Institut.

We intend to provide the following:

- Venue for the premiere concert of "Kolot — The Voices: Home" (90-minute programme)
- Technical infrastructure: sound system, lighting, and stage facilities
- Promotion of the event through our channels and local media partnerships
- Box office management and audience development

We look forward to welcoming "Kolot — The Voices: Home" to our stage.

Sincerely,

Krzysztof Kobyliński
Prezes, Fundacja Integracji Kultury
Centrum Kultury Jazovia
Gliwice, Poland
