To Whom It May Concern,

I, Krzysztof Ścierański, hereby confirm my intention to participate as a featured artist in the project "Kolot — The Voices: Home," an international music coproduction led by Dima Gorelik (Warsaw, Poland), in partnership with Berlin-based composer and percussionist Tom Dayan, supported by the International Coproduction Fund (IKF) of the Goethe-Institut.

I have been performing regularly with Dima Gorelik for several years and consider his work — particularly the methodology of "soft cultural displacement" — to be one of the most compelling artistic propositions in Polish contemporary jazz today. His ability to connect seemingly distant musical traditions resonates strongly with my own artistic experience spanning four decades on the international stage.

My involvement in the project will include:

- Participation in studio development sessions as a contributing artist
- Performance at selected concert presentations of the programme in Poland
- Artistic consultation on the programme's bass arrangements and ensemble dynamics

I look forward to contributing to this project and believe it represents a significant addition to the intercultural music landscape in Poland and beyond.

Sincerely,

Krzysztof Ścierański
Bassist, Composer
Warsaw, Poland
