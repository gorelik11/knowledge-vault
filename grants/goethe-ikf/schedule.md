# Schedule — Three Phases
## Kolot — The Voices: Home
## Estimated project duration: 15 months (July 2026 – September 2027)

---

### Phase 1: Creative Development (July – December 2026)
**"Artistic Laboratory"**

- Completion of string quartet arrangements in collaboration with Tom Dayan (Berlin ↔ Warsaw, remote + in-person sessions)
- Studio sessions in Warsaw: recording of overdubs (additional instruments, string quartet), experimentation with ensemble configurations
- Artistic decisions: selection of compositions for the live concert programme from the full repertoire of 23 pieces
- Preparation of performance scores and parts for live presentation
- Completion of the music video (Warsaw waltz — "Wawawaltz")
- Online presentation of artistic materials: release of singles, video content
- Interviews and media outreach (press, radio broadcasts, online platforms)

### Phase 2: Rehearsal and Programme Integration (January – April 2027)
**"Ensemble Residencies"**

- Rehearsal residency in Warsaw: full ensemble rehearsals integrating string quartet arrangements into the live programme
- Rehearsal residency in Berlin: working sessions with Tom Dayan, refining programme dramaturgy and concert arc
- Finalisation of the 90-minute concert programme as a cohesive performative experience
- Coordination and confirmation of presentation dates at venues in Poland and Germany
- Production of promotional materials for venues

### Phase 3: Presentations and Documentation (May – September 2027)
**"Public Presentations"**

- Premiere presentation at Centrum Kultury Jazovia, Gliwice (Fundacja Integracji Kultury)
- Presentations at Galeria Projekt (Warsaw), Teatr BOTO (Sopot), and Shlott (Berlin)
- 5-minute film documentation of the creative process and presentations
- Project evaluation and final reporting

## Notes:
